## ⚡ Bolt.new Prompt — Café Stock & Sales App

**Role:**
Build a simple full-stack café management app.

**Goal:**
Track **daily sales, stock, and revenue targets** to reach **NPR 200,000 per quarter**.

---

## Tech Stack

* Frontend: **React + Tailwind**
* Backend: **Node.js + Express**
* Database: **MongoDB (Mongoose)**
* API: REST

---

## Pages / Features

### 1. Dashboard

Show:

* Today’s sales
* Weekly sales vs target (15,500)
* Monthly sales vs target (66,700)
* Quarterly progress vs target (200,000)
* Top 5 selling items

Use progress bars and simple charts.

---

### 2. Sales Management

Form to add sales:

* Date (default today)
* Item
* Quantity
* Unit price
* Auto-calculate total

List daily sales.

---

### 3. Stock Management

For each item:

* Opening stock
* Purchased
* Sold (auto from sales)
* Wastage
* Closing stock (calculated)
* Cost price
* Selling price
* Margin %

Highlight:

* Low stock
* Margin below 40%

---

### 4. Menu Items

* Add / edit items
* Category (snack, drink, main)
* Activate / deactivate items

---

## MongoDB Models

**Item**

```js
name, category, costPrice, sellingPrice, minStock, isActive
```

**Sale**

```js
date, itemId, quantity, unitPrice, total
```

**Stock**

```js
itemId, openingStock, purchased, sold, wastage, closingStock, date
```

---

## Business Rules

* Closing stock = opening + purchased − sold − wastage
* Margin % = (selling − cost) / selling
* Quarterly total auto-calculated from sales
